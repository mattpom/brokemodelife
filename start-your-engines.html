🚗 Start Your Engines

Because every frugal warrior deserves to drive like a legend.

⛽ Gas Hacks

  • Use GasBuddy to track cheapest fuel in real-time.
  • Refuel early morning for denser gas = more miles.
  • Keep tires inflated and remove junk from your trunk — weight costs gas.

🧰 Repair Like a Rebel

  • AutoZone does engine code scans free — don’t pay a mechanic for this.
  • Order parts online and have your mechanic install — huge savings.
  • Duct tape + bungee cords = emergency bumper fix kit.

🍔 Drive-Thru Life

  • Taco Bell's Drive-Thru Dice Game on Tuesdays — spin, win, save.
  • Speedway 50¢ hot dog days: max 4 per customer.
  • Sheetz has half-price sliders after 8pm (app exclusive).

🧼 Detail On a Dime

  • DIY carwash kits from Dollar Tree = $5 showroom finish.
  • Old toothbrush + vinegar: unbeatable for interior grime.
  • Use newspaper for streak-free window shine.
