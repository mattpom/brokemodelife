🥡 No Waste Strategy

Plan meals backward from perishables. Leftover rice becomes stir fry. Roasted veg becomes soup. Soft bananas become smoothies. Nothing in the trash.

🏷️ Buffet Hacks

  • Label leftovers — masking tape and sharpie, write the date every time
  • Pre-split into containers — dinner becomes grab-and-go instantly
  • Frozen garlic cubes — saves 20 minutes of prep per week
  • Shop bulk with a friend — split a 10 lb bag of oats, both win
  • Weekly tray bake — one starch, one protein, three veggies, one oven session covers 3 meals
