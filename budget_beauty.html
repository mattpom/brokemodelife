🛒 WHERE TO SHOP AND SAVE

  • CVS clearance bins — check every visit, stock rotates constantly
  • Marshalls beauty aisle — name brands at 50-70% off
  • Amazon Subscribe and Save — 15% off repeat staples like razors and moisturizer
  • Ulta 21 Days of Beauty — twice a year, prestige products go 50% off
  • Target Circle app — stack manufacturer coupons with Circle offers

🧖 DIY SKINCARE THAT WORKS

  • Oatmeal and honey mask — gentle exfoliant, soothing, works for all skin types
  • Green tea toner — brew, cool, apply with cotton pad, antioxidants for pennies
  • Sugar and coconut oil scrub — lips and body, soft skin at zero cost
  • Aloe vera from the plant — better than any $40 after-sun gel
