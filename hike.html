🥾 Take a Hike – Trails, Parks & Free Nature Escapes

🌲 Top Free Hiking Trails in the U.S.:

  • Great Smoky Mountains National Park – NC/TN border
  • Zion National Park – Scenic views & slot canyons
  • Glacier National Park – MT alpine lakes & wildlife
  • Red River Gorge – KY sandstone arches & cliffs

🎒 Budget Hiking Essentials

  • Walmart hydration pack ($15)
  • Granola + peanut butter = trail energy
  • Good shoes? Try clearance running shoes from Ross or Marshalls

🌿 Tips for Broke Hikers

  • 💡 Use the AllTrails app (free version)
  • 🚻 Look for park trails with free parking & bathrooms
  • 📸 Bring your phone – some parks offer photo contests
  • 🗓 Visit during off-peak hours to avoid fees
