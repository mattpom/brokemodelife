🚫 Do As I Say, Not As I Do

A Broke Babe’s Guide to Common Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

🙊 Saying Yes to Everything

What They Do: Overcommit and get overwhelmed.

What to Do Instead: Learn to say “no” with grace. Protect your time like your last $20.

💸 Spending Without Tracking

What They Do: Swipe and hope it works out.

What to Do Instead: Track your spending weekly. Even broke mode needs a budget.

📱 Mindless Scrolling

What They Do: Wake up on TikTok, fall asleep on Instagram.

What to Do Instead: Set app limits. Use downtime for self-upgrades or real rest — except when you’re on BrokeModeLife.com (that’s practically self-care).

😤 Ignoring Your Body

What They Do: Skip sleep, ignore stress, binge caffeine.

What to Do Instead: Hydrate, stretch, breathe. Your body pays your bills. Treat it like gold.

🔄 Breaking Promises to Yourself

What They Do: “I’ll start Monday.” Every Monday.

What to Do Instead: Keep your word to yourself first. The rest will follow.
