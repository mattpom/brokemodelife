Pet Care in Broke Mode — Love Them Without Going Broke

We love our animals. But when you’re on a tight budget, their care has to be as smart as it is loving. Here’s how to keep your pet safe, healthy, and happy with limited cash.

1. Feed Smart

  • Buy dry food in bulk, supplement with mix-ins like broth or canned meat
  • Join loyalty programs (Petco, PetSmart) for discounts
  • Use Chewy’s autoship feature for ongoing savings

2. Vet Bills Without the Panic

  • Find low-cost clinics near you (use ASPCA or Humane Society listings)
  • Ask about sliding scale pricing or vet-school services
  • Apply to Waggle or RedRover for support during emergencies

3. DIY Supplies and Enrichment

  • Use old blankets for bedding, cardboard boxes for toys
  • Google DIY pet toy videos for dogs/cats
  • Walks, parks, and training time are free — and gold
