This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Broke Mode Life uses them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with content.

How We Use Cookies

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for basic site functionality Session
Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand traffic and improve content Up to 2 years
Affiliate Amazon Associates and other affiliate tracking 24–90 days
Preferences Remembers settings like dismissed banners 30 days

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services we use (such as Google Analytics and Amazon affiliate tracking) may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to those companies' privacy policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of this site. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies currently stored
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close the browser
