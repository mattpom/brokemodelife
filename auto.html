🚗 DIY Car Maintenance Tips

  • Change your own oil — save $40-$70 each time.
  • Check and refill fluids (brake, transmission, coolant).
  • Use YouTube to replace headlights, air filters, and wipers.

🛠️ Budget Auto Tools You Should Own

  • OBD-II scanner — diagnose check engine lights for under $30.
  • Portable tire inflator — avoid gas station fees.
  • Socket wrench set — basics for under-the-hood work.

⛽ Apps to Save on Gas

  • GasBuddy – Find cheapest stations near you.
  • Upside – Earn cashback on gas purchases.
  • GetUpside + Grocery Points – Stack rewards.

🔧 Frugal Auto Repair Resources

  • RepairPal – Estimate auto repair costs fairly.
  • YourMechanic – Mobile repair services.
  • YouTube Mechanics – Find tutorials by car model.
