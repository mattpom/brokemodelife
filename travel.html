Flight Hacks

Pay Less to Get There

✦ Always True

Five Moves That Cut Your Flight Cost Significantly

  • Google Flights Explore map — Click Explore and enter your home airport. It shows the cheapest destinations available right now. Let price decide where you go.
  • Fly Tuesday and Wednesday — Statistically 20 to 30 percent cheaper than weekends. Flexibility is the single best travel hack you have available.
  • Book 6 to 8 weeks out for domestic — The sweet spot for US flights. Too early and prices are inflated. Too late and they spike again before departure.
  • Carry-on only every time — Baggage fees average $35 to $70 per bag per flight. Master packing light and save $100 or more on every round trip.
  • Set price alerts on Google Flights — Click the bell icon on any route. Google emails you when the price drops. Check it, book it, done.

💡 The best travel hack is going where it is cheap right now, not where your bucket list says.

Free Accommodation

Sleep for Nothing

✦ Always True

How to Travel Without Paying for a Hotel

  • TrustedHousesitters — Connect with homeowners who need someone to watch their home. You stay free in exchange for caring for the place and pets.
  • Extended hotel stays negotiate — Staying 7 or more nights? Call and ask for a weekly rate. Most hotels give 20 to 30 percent off to guests who simply ask.
  • Loyalty programs add up fast — Marriott, Hilton, and IHG give free nights after enough points. Use one travel card for all spending and redeem annually.
  • Airbnb long-stay discounts — Booking 28 or more days unlocks monthly pricing, often 40 to 60 percent less than nightly rates in the same city.
  • Housesit internationally — TrustedHousesitters has listings in 130 countries. Travel the world and pay only for flights.

💡 Accommodation is often the biggest travel cost. Eliminate it and you can afford to go almost anywhere.
