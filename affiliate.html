What is an Affiliate Link?

An affiliate link is a special URL that tracks whether a purchase was referred from our site. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.

Our Affiliate Partners

Our Promise to You

We only recommend products and services we genuinely believe provide value. Affiliate relationships do not influence editorial decisions. If something doesn't meet our broke-mode standard — functional, worth the price, and honest — we won't recommend it regardless of commission potential.

In accordance with FTC guidelines, we disclose material affiliate relationships on any page or post where affiliate links appear. The presence of affiliate links does not imply endorsement of any specific retailer or platform beyond what is stated.
