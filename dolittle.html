Dr. Dolittle’s Autobiography

Owning a pet shouldn’t be ruff
🦴 Budget-Friendly Food Brands:
  • Chewy.com - Autoship discounts up to 35% off.
  • Walmart - House brand foods like Pure Balance and Ol’ Roy.
  • Consider rotating protein sources to save while keeping pets healthy.
🐾 DIY Pet Toys & Games:
  • Use old socks + tennis balls for instant tug toys.
  • Freeze treats in muffin trays or ice cube molds for mental stimulation.
  • Cardboard boxes = cat castles on a budget.
💊 Affordable Pet Meds:
  • 1-800-PetMeds – Generic meds often 50% cheaper.
  • Ask your vet for written prescriptions to shop around.
  • Some human meds are safe for pets (check dosage with vet).
🐕 Grooming at Home:
  • Use baby wipes for quick paw & fur cleanups.
  • DIY shampoo: 1 part dish soap, 1 part vinegar, 2 parts water.
  • Nail grinders are safer than clippers and cost $20 or less.
🏥 Vet Savings Hacks:
  • Look for low-cost vaccine clinics at Petco or local shelters.
  • Banfield Wellness Plans = subscription care at PetSmart locations.
  • Ask about bundle pricing for multiple pets or annual care.
🛒 Discount Pet Supplies:
  • Dollar Tree - Bowls, leashes, waste bags.
  • Marshalls & TJ Maxx - Pet beds, toys, gear.
  • Amazon - Subscribe & Save discounts.
