🎧 Are You Listening? – Stream & Listen Broke

Explore top free or cheap options for streaming music and podcasts without breaking the bank.

🎶 Music Streaming Services

  • Spotify Free – Ad-supported with access to millions of tracks
  • Pandora – Personalized internet radio with a free tier
  • YouTube Music Free – Stream with occasional ads
  • Amazon Music (Ad-Supported)
  • SoundCloud – Tons of indie tracks & remixes
  • Deezer Free – International music variety
  • Tubi – Includes music video channels

🎙️ Podcast Platforms

  • Spotify Podcasts – Huge catalog of free shows
  • Apple Podcasts – Native on all iPhones, totally free
  • Google Podcasts – Clean interface, no cost
  • iHeartRadio – News, comedy, music, and more
  • Stitcher – Great for niche and indie shows
  • TuneIn – Includes live radio and podcast shows
  • Audible Free Tier – Select podcasts without subscription
