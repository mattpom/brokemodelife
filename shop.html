🧠 Think Function, Not Flash

Looks are cheap. Utility pays rent. Do you need a $70 smart mug or will a $4 thermos do the job? Ask that question before you click Buy. The best purchases are invisible — you just use them every day and they work.

⚡ Broke Mode Shopping Rules

  • Read 1-star reviews first — truth lives there, not in the 5-stars
  • Check Amazon Warehouse — open-box at 20-40% off, often functionally new
  • Use CamelCamelCamel — tracks Amazon price history, alerts you when prices drop
  • Buy open box at Best Buy — same product, lower price, real return window
  • Never buy cables at retail — Amazon basics cables work identically for 80% less
  • Upgrade with texture, not furniture — throw pillows, lighting, and wall hooks flip a space for under $10
