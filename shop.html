Shop Smart in Broke Mode — Every Dollar Counts

When you're in Broke Mode, shopping isn’t casual — it’s war. You don’t just spend. You plan, stack, and hack. This article breaks down how to buy what you need, avoid what you don’t, and squeeze every discount available.

1. Always Stack Savings

  • Use browser extensions: Rakuten, Honey, Capital One Shopping
  • Combine cashback apps: Ibotta, Fetch, Upside (gas)
  • Look for promo codes: RetailMeNot, SlickDeals, Reddit forums

2. Buy Smart — Not Just Cheap

  • Look for refurbished: BackMarket, Woot!, and eBay outlet stores
  • Buy generic store brands (they often use the same supplier)
  • Use price trackers for essentials (CamelCamelCamel, Keepa)

3. Time Your Purchases

  • January = fitness, bedding; March = tax-time electronics
  • Shop clearance days: end of seasons or holidays
  • Sign up for store rewards and wait for double-points promos
