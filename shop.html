Five Rules That Save Real Money Every Time You Shop

  • Read 1-star reviews first — That is where the truth lives. If they all say broke in 2 weeks, believe them over the paid 5-star reviews every time.
  • CamelCamelCamel before Amazon — Check price history before buying anything. Most items cycle on sale every 4 to 6 weeks. Wait and save 20 to 40 percent.
  • Amazon Warehouse is real — Open-box items at 20 to 40 percent off. Grade A condition usually means someone returned it unopened or barely used.
  • The 72-hour rule on anything over $50 — If you still want it after three days it is probably worth it. If you forgot about it, you did not need it at all.
  • Return policy is part of the purchase — Only buy from sellers with easy no-questions returns. If it breaks or disappoints, send it back and protect every dollar.

💡 The best purchase is the one you did not make impulsively. Patience is the most profitable shopping strategy you have.
