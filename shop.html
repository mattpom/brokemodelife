⚡ BROKE MODE SHOPPING RULES

  • Read 1-star reviews first — truth lives there, not in the 5-stars
  • Check Amazon Warehouse — open-box at 20-40% off, often functionally new
  • Use CamelCamelCamel — tracks Amazon price history, alerts you to drops
  • Buy open box at Best Buy — same product, lower price, real return window
  • Never buy cables at retail price — basics cables work identically for 80% less
  • Upgrade with texture not furniture — wall hooks and throw pillows flip a space for under $10
