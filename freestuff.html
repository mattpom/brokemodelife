🧠 The Broke Mode Freebie System

  • Burner email only — keep your real inbox clean. One address for all freebie signups.
  • Build your freebie route — coffee in the morning, smoothie by lunch, sample after dinner. Stack it.
  • Track in a simple spreadsheet — note expiration dates so you don't miss claim windows.
  • r/freebies on Reddit — community posts real deals daily. Fast-moving, worth checking weekly.
  • Library card = entertainment budget solved — Libby, Kanopy, Hoopla. Books, movies, music. All free.
