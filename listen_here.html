Free Streaming

Get All the Music for Nothing

✦ Always True

Five Platforms That Cost Zero Dollars

  • Spotify Free is genuinely fine — Shuffle mode and occasional ads is a small price for 100 million songs. Use it on WiFi and your data stays flat all month.
  • YouTube Music free tier — No subscription required for full catalog access. The ads are short and the library includes tracks Spotify does not have.
  • Libby for free audiobooks — Connect your library card to the Libby app. Thousands of audiobooks and podcasts delivered completely free to your phone.
  • SoundCloud for discovery — Independent artists, early releases, underground scenes. Content you literally cannot find on the major platforms.
  • TuneIn for live radio worldwide — Every radio station on earth, completely free. Sports, news, music, and talk without a single subscription required.

💡 The best music is the music that moves you. Almost all of it is free if you know where to look.

Podcast Picks

Knowledge That Pays You Back

✦ Always True

Five Free Shows Worth Every Minute

  • Planet Money — NPR — Economics explained clearly and engagingly. Every episode changes how you see money and everyday financial decisions.
  • How I Built This — NPR — Founders of iconic companies tell the real story. Free business school that actually holds your attention the whole way through.
  • Huberman Lab — Stanford neuroscientist breaks down health, sleep, and performance. Free science you can apply the same day you hear it.
  • Freakonomics Radio — Why things work the way they do. Every episode rewires how you think about incentives, behavior, and everyday systems.
  • Darknet Diaries — True stories from hackers and the dark side of the internet. More educational than it sounds and wildly bingeable.

💡 A good podcast during a commute or workout turns dead time into a genuine competitive advantage. All completely free.
