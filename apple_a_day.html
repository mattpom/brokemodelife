Eat Clean, Stay Sharp — the Broke Mode Way

Welcome to Apple a Day — your survival guide for staying healthy, alert, and energized without blowing your budget. Broke Mode health isn't about gym memberships, $12 smoothies, or boutique supplements. It’s about precision. Simplicity. Efficiency. And that’s exactly what you’ll find here.

We break wellness into four essential pillars: food, movement, stress, and sleep. No gimmicks. Just systems that work — even if your budget’s running on fumes.

1. Eat With Intent

  • Bulk is king: oats, rice, beans, lentils, frozen vegetables. These foods fuel your body and stretch your dollar.
  • Meal hack: rotate 3–4 staple dishes each week. Example: baked sweet potatoes, chickpea stew, overnight oats, and rice bowls.
  • Smart additions: canned salmon, eggs, peanut butter — protein-dense and budget-friendly.

2. Move Daily — For Free

  • Use YouTube workouts (HIIT, yoga, calisthenics)
  • Walk everywhere. Track steps with your phone. Challenge yourself weekly.
  • Use resistance bands or water jugs as home workout tools.

3. Control Stress

  • Daily brain dump: write 3–5 things every morning to clear your mind.
  • Use the Libby app for free guided meditations and audiobooks.
  • Protect your peace: create a “No Chaos” zone in your day — even 15 minutes matters.

4. Sleep with Strategy

  • Power down electronics 30 minutes before bed.
  • Use fan noise, brown noise, or sleep podcasts (search “Sleep With Me” or “Nothing Much Happens”)
  • Stick to a routine: same sleep/wake time every day builds rhythm and energy.
