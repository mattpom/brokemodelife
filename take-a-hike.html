🥾 Take a Hike

Backyard hikes to national parks — your budget boots were made for walkin'.

Free Parks

  • State parks have no-fee days.
  • Library cards often come with free passes.
  • Follow local hiking clubs for group hikes.

Daypack Essentials

  • Snacks > gear.
  • Trail map screenshot saves data.
  • Duct tape for surprise blisters.

Frugal Fun

  • Sunrise selfies are free.
  • Sketch the scenery instead of snapping.
  • BYO mug = hot trail tea moment.
